About Dropoff DMV

Your local solution for marketplace pickups and deliveries

Our Story

Dropoff DMV was born from a simple observation: buying items on Facebook Marketplace and other online platforms is easy, but getting them home can be a challenge.

As residents of Columbia, MD, we noticed that many great deals were being missed because people didn't have the means to transport larger items, couldn't coordinate pickup times with sellers, or simply didn't want to deal with the hassle of meeting strangers.

In 2025, we launched Dropoff DMV with a mission to make local marketplace shopping more accessible and convenient for everyone in our community. What started as a simple delivery service has grown into a trusted local business that connects buyers with their purchases quickly and reliably.

Our Mission

At Dropoff DMV, our mission is to make local marketplace shopping as convenient as possible by providing reliable, affordable, and friendly delivery services.
